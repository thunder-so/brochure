Thunder allows you to manage multiple AWS accounts and regions effortlessly. This feature is particularly useful for users working with diverse environments or projects spread across different AWS accounts or regions.

Enter an alias and click on the button to navigate to AWS Cloudformation.

At the bottom of the page, click the checkbox and click the <code>Create stack</code> button.

<img src="/screens/cloudformation-stack.png" alt="Add an AWS Account" class="mx-auto w-auto h-auto">

When your stack is successfully installed, return to Thunder to find your account connected.

<img src="/screens/aws-account.png" alt="Connected AWS Account" class="mx-auto w-auto h-auto">

You are now ready to deploy on AWS.
