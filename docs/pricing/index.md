Our pricing is Organization-based, not Usage-based. We sell software, not seats or artificial limits on your apps or infra. Start a `30-day free trial` of Thunder and then pay a fair and fixed monthly price.

Your AWS usage costs remain your responsibility.

Solo
- A default Org will be created during onboarding. "Personal Projects"
- Unlimited commercial use 
- No limits on Apps, Environments, Deploys
- Community Support

$0/mo/org, forever free
Sign up with Github

Team
- +New Org with payment flow
- All Personal features included
- Github Organizations, Collaborators
- Invite Team Members, no limit 
- Multiple AWS Connections
- Account Manager

$25/mo/org
Try 30 days free

Early Adopter
- All features unlocked
- Priority Support

$249 one-time, lifetime license.
Pay $249

Enterprise
- SOC2
- SSO/SAML for Thunder Console
- Designated Account Manager
- Priority email support & SLAs
- Private Slack channel
Contact us

All plans include
- 100% data ownership 
- Forever data retention
- Privacy law compliance
- Enterprise-grade infrastructure
- No vendor lock-in. Keep everything working when you cancel.

All pricing is in USD and renews automatically unless cancelled.
